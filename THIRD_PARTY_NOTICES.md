## Coolicons

NightWatch's local SVG icon vocabulary adapts selected shapes and conventions
from Coolicons by Kryston Schwarze. Coolicons is licensed under Creative
Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).

- Source: https://github.com/krystonschwarze/coolicons
- License: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

The icons remain embedded as dependency-free, `currentColor` SVG paths so
NightWatch themes, accessibility states, and offline builds do not depend on a
runtime icon service.
